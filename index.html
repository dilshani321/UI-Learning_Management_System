EVENTS AND ACTIVITIES


Educational Visit to 'Diyasaru Uyana’, Battaramulla
Students of the Department of Environmental Technology visited the ‘Diyasaru Uyana’ which is a well-functioning urban wetland park under the Sri Lanka Land Development Cooperation, Battaramulla on 1st of November 2019. The field visit covered several practical aspects related to the Fundamentals of Ecology course module which is followed by the first-year students attached to the Department of Environmental Technology.
Four-day field excursion
A four days field visit to Negombo, Giritale, and Polonnaruwa had been organized by the Department of Environmental Technology from 8th to 11th November 2019, covering the practical sessions on the Analytical techniques for studying water samples, Water pollution, Ecology, and Conservation biology course modules. Students were able to engage with field practicals and laboratory experiments gaining exposure to field applications related to the above course modules.
Educational Visit to Industrial Park, Avissawella
Students of the Department of Environmental Technology visited Teejay Lanka PLC at the Seethawaka Export Processing Zone, Industrial Park, Avissawella on the 21st of December 2018. The purpose of this visit was to study the waste management procedures during industrial activities under the Biology for Environmental Technology course module.
